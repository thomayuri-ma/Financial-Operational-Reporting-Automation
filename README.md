# Financial & Operational Reporting Automation

This project demonstrates an automated financial and operational reporting workflow designed to support sales, finance, and business operations teams.

---

## Problem Statement
Finance and operations teams often rely on manual processes to prepare recurring sales and financial reports. These processes are time-consuming, error-prone, and make it difficult to track trends, returns, and variance against budgets.

---

## Solution
This project automates financial and operational reporting by:
- Consolidating sales and operational data
- Calculating net sales, returns, and budget variance
- Generating monthly performance summaries
- Producing ready-to-use reports for business stakeholders

---

## Key Features
- Automated data preparation and cleaning
- Monthly sales and financial summaries
- Trend analysis by channel and product category
- Variance analysis against budget
- Exportable reports for recurring use

---

## Tech Stack
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Jupyter / Google Colab

---

## Example Use Cases
- Monthly sales and finance reporting
- Budget vs actual analysis
- Operational performance tracking
- Decision support for finance and management teams

---

## How to Run
1. Open the notebook in Google Colab or Jupyter
2. Run the single cell to generate reports
3. Review exported CSV files for analysis and reporting
